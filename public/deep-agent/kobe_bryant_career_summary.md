# Kobe Bryant – Career Accomplishments Quick Reference

**NBA Championships**
- **5× Champion** – Los Angeles Lakers (2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010)
- **2× Finals MVP** – 2009, 2010

**Most Valuable Player (MVP) Awards**
- **Regular‑Season MVP:** None (runner‑up in 2008) 
- **Finals MVP:** 2 (see above)
- **All‑Star Game MVP:** 4 (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011)

**All‑Star Selections**
- **18× All‑Star** – 1998, 2000‑2016 (except 1999, when the game was cancelled)
- **All‑Star Game MVP:** 4 times (see above)

**All‑NBA & All‑Defensive Honors**
- **All‑NBA Teams:** 15 selections (4 First, 2 Second, 9 Third)
- **All‑Defensive Teams:** 12 selections (9 First, 3 Second)

**Scoring Records & Milestones**
- **Career points:** 33,643 (4th‑most in NBA history at retirement, currently 6th)
- **81‑point game:** Jan 22 2006 vs Toronto – second‑highest single‑game total ever.
- **60‑point farewell:** Apr 13 2016 – final NBA game.
- **30‑+ point games:** 1226 (most in NBA history)
- **20+ seasons** with the Lakers (1996‑2016).
- **30,000‑point club:** Joined in Dec 2015; youngest player to reach the milestone at the time.
- **30k/6k/6k club:** Surpassed 30,000 points, 6,000 rebounds and 6,000 assists.

**Other Notable Achievements**
- **Olympic Gold Medals:** 2008 (Beijing) & 2012 (London) – member of “Redeem Team”.
- **NBA Rookie of the Year:** 1997 (shared with Allen Iverson).
- **Number Retired:** #8 (Lakers, 2017) and #24 (Lakers, 2017).
- **Hall of Fame:** Inducted into Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame (2020, as part of the 2020 class).
- **Scoring Consistency:** Averaged ≥25 PPG in 12 seasons; 30+ PPG in 10 seasons.

**Key Career Highlights**
- **2000‑2002:** First three‑peat, becoming the youngest player to win three straight titles.
- **2006:** 81‑point performance, highest single‑game total since Wilt Chamberlain’s 100‑point game.
- **2009‑2010:** Back‑to‑back championships; first player since Shaquille O’Neal (2000‑01) to win Finals MVP in consecutive years.
- **2016:** Historic 60‑point final game, cementing his reputation as a clutch scorer.

*All figures are based on official NBA statistics.*
