# Kobe Bryant – Career Accomplishments Summary

## NBA Career
- **Teams:** Los Angeles Lakers (1996–2016)
- **Position:** Shooting Guard / Small Forward
- **NBA Championships:** 5 (2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010)
- **NBA Finals MVP:** 2 (2009, 2010)
- **Regular‑Season MVP:** 1 (2008)
- **All‑NBA Teams:** 15× (11× First Team, 2× Second Team, 2× Third Team)
- **All‑Star Appearances:** 18× (1998–2016)
- **All‑Star Game MVP:** 4× (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011)
- **NBA Scoring Champion:** 2× (2006, 2007)
- **Career Points:** 33,643 (4th all‑time at retirement)
- **Career Averages:** 25.0 PPG, 5.2 RPG, 4.7 APG
- **Notable Statistical Records**
  - Youngest player to reach 30,000 career points (34 years, 104 days).
  - First player in NBA history with 5,000 points, 1,500 rebounds, and 1,500 assists in a single season (2005‑06).
  - Holds the record for most points in a single game by a Laker (81 points, 2006).
  - 2nd most all‑time three‑point field goals made by a player who played his entire career with one team (1,827).
  - 8× NBA Player of the Week, 6× NBA Player of the Month.

## International Play
- **Olympic Gold Medals:** 2 (2008 Beijing, 2012 London) – member of the USA "Redeem Team" and "Dream Team".
- **FIBA World Cup:** Gold (2006) – part of the USA team.

## Milestones & Honors
- **Number Retired:** #8 (2006) and #24 (2017) – only player in NBA history to have two numbers retired by the same franchise.
- **Hall of Fame:** Inducted into Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame (2020, posthumously).
- **NBA 75th Anniversary Team:** Named to the 2021 NBA 75‑Greatest Players list.
- **Scoring Milestones:** 30,000‑point club (2012), 20,000‑point club (2007), 10,000‑point club (2003).
- **Other Records:** Most consecutive games scoring 20+ points (866), most seasons with 30+ points per game (5), most seasons with 25+ points per game (12).

## Off‑Court Achievements
### Philanthropy & Community
- **Mamba & Mambacita Sports Foundation:** Founded with his wife Vanessa to empower youth through sports and education; later renamed after his daughter Gianna.
- **Make‑A‑Wish Foundation:** Granted over 200 wishes for children with life‑threatening illnesses.
- **Youth Sports Initiatives:** Co‑creator of the "Mamba Sports Academy" (now "Mamba Sports Academy"), providing training programs for athletes.
- **Scholarships:** Established scholarships for under‑privileged students.

### Business & Media
- **Venture Capital:** Co‑founder of "Granity Studios," a multimedia company producing content across film, TV, and digital platforms.
- **Books:** Author of the best‑selling children’s book series "The Wizenard Series" and "Dear Basketball" (which won the 2018 Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film).
- **Documentary:** Executive producer of the Oscar‑winning short "Dear Basketball" and featured in the documentary "Kobe Bryant: The Last Season".
- **Investments:** Stakeholder in sports‑tech startups (e.g., BodyArmor, a sports‑drink brand) and partner in the NBA’s "League of Legends" e‑sports venture.
- **Brand Partnerships:** Long‑term ambassador for Nike, with the iconic Kobe line of basketball shoes.

### Honors & Recognitions
- **Academy Award (Oscar):** Best Animated Short Film for "Dear Basketball" (2018).
- **Time 100:** Listed among the 100 most influential people in the world (2015).
- **Presidential Medal of Freedom (posthumous):** Awarded by President Joe Biden (2022).
- **ESPN Sportsman of the Year (2015).**
