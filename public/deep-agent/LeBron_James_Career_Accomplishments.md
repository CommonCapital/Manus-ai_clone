# LeBron James – Career Accomplishments Summary

- **NBA Championships:** 4 (2012, 2013 with Miami Heat; 2016 with Cleveland Cavaliers; 2020 with Los Angeles Lakers)
- **NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) Awards:** 4 (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)
- **NBA Scoring Titles:** 1 (2008) – led the league with 30.0 points per game
- **NBA All‑Star Appearances:** 19 (2005–2023, excluding 2021 due to injury) – holds the record for most selections
- **Olympic Medals:** 2 – Gold (2008 Beijing, 2012 London) and Bronze (2004 Athens) as a member of Team USA
- **Notable Records & Achievements:
  - All‑time leader in playoff points (10,400+ as of 2024)
  - First player in NBA history with at least 30,000 career points, 10,000 rebounds, and 10,000 assists
  - Youngest player to reach 30,000 career points (33 years, 24 days)
  - Holds the record for most consecutive NBA Finals appearances (8, 2011‑2018)
  - Named to the All‑NBA First Team 13 times (record)
  - NBA Rookie of the Year (2004)
  - Multiple NBA All‑Defensive Team selections
  - Ranked #1 on the NBA’s 75‑Year Anniversary Team (2021)
