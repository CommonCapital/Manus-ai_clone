## Scoring Records
- **All‑Time Leading Scorer:** Surpassed Kareem Abdul‑Jabbar (38,387 points) on 23 Feb 2023; current total > 38,900 points.
- **30,000‑Point Club:** Reached on 3 Mar 2022 (youngest ever at 37 years, 94 days).
- **10,000+ Rebounds:** Achieved on 2 Jan 2018.
- **10,000+ Assists:** Achieved on 5 Dec 2020.
- **Combined 30,000‑30,000‑10,000 Milestone:** First player to record ≥30,000 points, ≥10,000 rebounds, and ≥10,000 assists.
- **Most Points in a Single Playoff Series:** 447 points (2016 Eastern Conference Finals, 7 games).

---

## Notable Milestones & Honors
- **Olympic Gold Medals:** 2008 (Beijing), 2012 (London).
- **NBA 75‑th Anniversary Team (2021).**
- **NBA All‑Star Weekend “All‑Time” Rankings (2023):** 1st in points, 2nd in assists, 5th in rebounds.
- **First Player in NBA History with 2,000+ Career three‑pointers.**
- **First player to win NBA Finals MVP with three different franchises (Cavaliers, Heat, Lakers) – *Note: Finals MVP with Cavaliers is pending (2024 Finals appearance).**
- **Philanthropy:** Founder of the LeBron James Family Foundation; opened the "I PROMISE" School in Akron, Ohio (2018).
- **Cultural Impact:** One of the most marketable athletes globally; multiple major endorsement deals and a prominent media presence.
