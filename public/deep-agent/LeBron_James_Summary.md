LeBron James is a four‑time NBA champion (2012, 2013 with Miami; 2016 with Cleveland; 2020 with Los Angeles) and a four‑time league MVP (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013). He has been selected to the All‑Star Game 19 times, ranking among the most‑capped players in NBA history. Internationally, James earned two Olympic gold medals (2008, 2012) and a bronze (2004) with Team USA. Notable records include being the youngest player to reach 30,000 career points, the all‑time NBA playoff points leader, and holding the most NBA Finals MVP awards (four).
