Michael Jordan – six-time NBA champion (1991‑1993, 1996‑1998); five regular‑season MVP awards (1988, 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998); fourteen All‑Star selections (1985‑1993, 1996‑1998, 2002‑2003); two Olympic gold medals (1984, 1992). Notable records include ten scoring titles, six Finals MVPs (most ever), a career scoring average of 30.1 points per game, a 63‑point game in the 1986 playoffs (record for most points in a playoff game), and a perfect 6‑0 record in NBA Finals.
