## Kobe Bryant – Career Accomplishments

- **NBA Championships:** 5 (Los Angeles Lakers: 2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010)
- **Regular‑Season MVP Awards:** 1 (2007‑08 season)
- **NBA Finals MVP Awards:** None (but was a key contributor in all five titles)
- **Scoring Titles:** 2 (2005‑06, 2006‑07 seasons) – led the league with 35.4 ppg and 31.6 ppg respectively
- **All‑Star Appearances:** 18 (1998, 2000–2016) – tied for most in NBA history at the time of retirement
- **All‑Star MVP Awards:** 4 (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011)
- **Olympic Medals:** 2 Gold Medals with Team USA (2008 Beijing, 2012 London)
- **Notable Records & Achievements:**
  - Scored **81 points** in a single game (Jan 2006) – second‑highest point total in NBA history.
  - Recorded **60 points** in Game 4 of the 2010 NBA Finals – most points ever in a Finals game.
  - Finished career with **33,643 points**, ranking 4th all‑time at retirement.
  - First player in NBA history to score **50+ points in six consecutive games** (Feb‑Mar 2007).
  - **4× All‑NBA First Team**, **3× All‑NBA Second Team** selections.
  - **9× NBA All‑Defensive Team** selections (3 First Team, 6 Second Team).
  - Holds the record for most **All‑Star Game selections** (18) and ties for most **All‑Star MVP awards** (4).
  - Inducted into the **Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame** (2018, as a player).
