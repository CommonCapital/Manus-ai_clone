# Michael Jordan – Career Accomplishments (Quick Reference)

## NBA Championships
- **6× Champion** – Chicago Bulls (1991‑1993, 1996‑1998)
- **6× NBA Finals MVP** – (1991‑1993, 1996‑1998) – perfect 6‑0 Finals record

## Regular‑Season MVP Awards
- **5× Most Valuable Player**
  - 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998 (season awards)  
  - *Note*: Jordan’s fifth MVP was awarded for the 1995‑96 season (often counted as 1996); the award years are commonly listed as 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998.

## All‑Star Selections
- **14× NBA All‑Star** (1985‑1993, 1996‑1998, 2002‑2003)
- **3× All‑Star Game MVP** (1988, 1996, 1998)

## Scoring Titles
- **10× NBA Scoring Champion**
  - Consecutive titles 1987‑1993 (7 straight) and 1996‑1998 (3 straight)
  - Career scoring average: **30.12 pts/game** – highest in NBA history

## Olympic Medals
- **2× Gold Medalist** – Team USA
  - 1984 Los Angeles (college‑level team)
  - 1992 Barcelona ("Dream Team")

## Notable Records & Milestones
- **Career Points:** 32,292 (5th all‑time) – 30.12 pts/game (all‑time leader)
- **Most Points in a Playoff Game:** 63 (Boston Celtics, 1986)
- **Most Points in a Regular‑Season Game:** 69 (Cleveland Cavaliers, 1990)
- **NBA Defensive Player of the Year:** 1988
- **9× All‑Defensive First Team**, **1× All‑Defensive Second Team**
- **30‑point games:** 31 seasons with 30+ points; 40+ games with 30+ points (1986‑87 season)
- **First player to win MVP, Finals MVP, and All‑Star Game MVP in the same season** (1996)
- **Inducted into Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame:** 2009 (individual) & 2010 (as part of 1992 Dream Team)

---
*All figures are accurate as of the end of Jordan’s playing career (2003).*
