# Michael Jordan – Career Accomplishments (Quick Reference)

## NBA Championships
- **6× NBA Champion** – Chicago Bulls (1991, 1992, 1993, 1996, 1997, 1998)
- **6× NBA Finals MVP** – (1991‑1993, 1996‑1998) – record for most Finals MVPs in a career.

## MVP Awards
- **5× NBA Most Valuable Player** – 1988, 1991, 1992, 1996, 1998
- **1× NBA All‑Star Game MVP** – 1988, 1996, 1998 (three times)  
- **2× NBA All‑Defensive First Team** – 1988, 1990–1993 (four selections)  
- **9× All‑NBA First Team** – 1987–1993, 1996–1998

## All‑Star Selections
- **14× NBA All‑Star** – 1985–1993, 1996–1998, 2002, 2003 (14 total)
- **3× All‑Star Game MVP** – 1988, 1996, 1998

## Scoring Records
- **10× NBA Scoring Champion** – 1987–1993, 1996–1998 (record) 
- **Career scoring average:** 30.12 p.p.g. (highest in NBA history) 
- **Highest single‑season scoring average:** 37.1 p.p.g. (1986‑87) 
- **Most points in a single game:** 69 (vs. Cleveland, 1990) 
- **Career points:** 32,292 (5th all‑time as of 2024) 
- **Most points in a playoff series:** 246 (1992 NBA Finals, 5 games) 

## Notable Milestones & Honors
- **Olympic Gold Medalist** – 1984 (USA) and 1992 (Dream Team) 
- **NBA Rookie of the Year** – 1985 
- **NBA 75th Anniversary Team** – named to the 2021 "NBA 75" list 
- **Hall of Fame Induction** – Basketball Hall of Fame (2009) and FIBA Hall of Fame (2015) 
- **Career Win‑Loss Record (Regular Season):** 706‑263 (≈73% winning %) 
- **Record‑setting 1995‑96 season** – 72‑10 regular‑season record (most wins in a season at the time) 
- **First player to win MVP, Finals MVP, and All‑Star MVP in the same season** – 1996 
- **Retired jersey #23 (Chicago Bulls) and #45 (briefly) – #23 later re‑retired** 
- **Business & cultural impact:** Global brand ambassador, owner of Charlotte Hornets, and influential figure in sports marketing.
