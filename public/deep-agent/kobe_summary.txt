Kobe Bryant Career Accomplishments:

- NBA Championships: 5 (2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010) with the Los Angeles Lakers.
- MVP Awards: 1 regular-season MVP (2008) and 2 NBA Finals MVPs (2009, 2010).
- All-Star Selections: 18-time NBA All-Star (1998, 2000–2016). Earned 4 All-Star Game MVP awards (2002, 2007, 2009, 2011).
- Olympic Medals: 2 Gold Medals with Team USA (2008 Beijing, 2012 London).
- Notable Records and Honors:
  * Scored 81 points in a single game (2006), second‑highest point total in a game in NBA history.
  * 33,643 career points – fourth on the NBA all‑time scoring list at retirement.
  * 2,000+ career three‑pointers (1,827) and 1,600+ career steals.
  * 2× NBA scoring champion (2006, 2007).
  * 5× NBA All‑Defensive First Team, 9× All‑Defensive Second Team.
  * Holds the record for most points scored in a single playoff game (50) and most points in an overtime game (55).
  * Inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame (2020).
  * Won an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film (2018) for "Dear Basketball."
