LeBron James Career Accomplishments:

NBA Championships: 4 (2012, 2013 with Miami Heat; 2016 with Cleveland Cavaliers; 2020 with Los Angeles Lakers)

NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) Awards: 4 (2009, 2010, 2012, 2013)

NBA All-Star Selections: 19 (2005–2023, 2025) – holds the record for most All-Star selections.

Olympic Medals: 2 Gold Medals (2008 Beijing, 2012 London) and 1 Bronze Medal (2004 Athens) as a member of Team USA.

Notable Records and Achievements:
- All-time leading scorer in NBA history (as of 2024).
- Only player in NBA history with at least 30,000 points, 10,000 rebounds, and 10,000 assists.
- Youngest player to reach 30,000 career points.
- Holds the record for most playoff points (7,500+).
- 4× NBA Finals MVP.
- 2× NBA Scoring Champion (2008, 2023).
- 13× All-NBA First Team selections (record).
- 6× NBA All-Defensive Team selections.
- Named to the NBA 75th Anniversary Team.
- Holds numerous franchise records with the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers.
- Known for his versatility, durability, and leadership on and off the court.
